PIKACHU

The Electric Mouse Pokémon!

Pika Pika!

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon and the mascot of the Pokémon franchise. Known for its cute appearance and powerful electric abilities, Pikachu is the best friend and partner of Ash Ketchum in the Pokémon anime series. When Pikachu is happy or surprised, it releases electricity uncontrollably!

Pokémon Data

  • Type: Electric
  • Height: 0.4 m (1'04")
  • Weight: 6.0 kg (13.2 lbs)
  • Abilities: Static (may paralyze on contact)
  • Hidden Ability: Lightning Rod
  • Evolves From: Pichu (with high friendship)
  • Evolves Into: Raichu (with Thunder Stone)

Moves & Abilities

  • ⚡ Thunderbolt (Signature move!)
  • 💥 Quick Attack
  • 🌀 Electro Ball
  • 🏃 Iron Tail
  • 🛡️ Volt Tackle
  • 🌟 Gigantamax Form (in Sword/Shield)

Why Pikachu is My Favorite

Pikachu is the most iconic Pokémon for a reason! Its cute design, powerful electric attacks, and loyal personality make it the perfect companion. I love how Pikachu can be both adorable and fierce in battle. The bond between Ash and Pikachu in the anime is truly inspiring!
